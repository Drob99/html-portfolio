The Best Movies According to Omar

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Kung Fu Panda

Great family-friendly movies with enticing plot and amazing characters.

Harry Potter Movies

A classic story about a boy finding a new side about himself. Great plot and brilliant acting especially Alan Rickman.

War Horse

Following the story of a horse and how he changes from one owner to another only to return to his original one in a touching way.
